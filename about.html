About ShoreSquad

Empowering communities to protect our coastal environments

Our Mission

ShoreSquad is dedicated to mobilizing young people and communities to keep our beaches clean while fostering environmental awareness and social connections. We believe that collective action, supported by technology and community engagement, can make a lasting impact on our coastal environments.

Our Values

Community First

Building strong connections between eco-conscious individuals and fostering a sense of shared responsibility.

Environmental Impact

Taking meaningful action to protect our coastal ecosystems and marine life through regular cleanup initiatives.

Inclusive Action

Creating opportunities for everyone to participate in environmental conservation, regardless of experience.

Innovation

Using technology to make beach cleanups more accessible, efficient, and engaging for all participants.
